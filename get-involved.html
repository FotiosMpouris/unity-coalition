Reaching Out to Other Groups

One of our main objectives is to collaborate with like-minded organizations. If you know a local group or national network that might align with the Unity Coalition’s goals, consider:

  1. Sharing our mission and website.
  2. Inviting them to schedule a meet-and-greet or virtual call.
  3. Coordinating a joint event or workshop.
  4. Pooling resources to address specific shared goals.

The more groups we connect with, the closer we come to genuine civic revival.
