Our Mission

Our mission is to unite and coordinate civic-minded influencers, organizations, and citizens around shared goals. We aim to reform and revitalize the very core of American civic life by...

  • Establishing a robust legal framework (501c3 and 501c4)
  • Building a powerful online platform (e.g., Mighty Networks)
  • Fostering collaboration across ten critical high-level topic areas
  • Encouraging new party structures to crystallize around a shared vision
